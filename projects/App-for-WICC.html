App for WICC

Over two summers during high school, I worked on completely designing and developing an iOS app, written in XCode and Swift, for the organization Women in Computing at Cornell, an organization in my hometown that works on supporting women in CS throughout the community.

Features of the app include a central calendar displaying events from multiple Google Calendars the organization operates, customizable push notifications and filters, and Google Sign-in with OAuth to display additional material for executive board members.
